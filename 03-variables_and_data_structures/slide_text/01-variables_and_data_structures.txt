Keywords/Functions generally need something to act upon. Data.

You can either have hard coded 'use once' data, or 'keep it around and manipulate' data.

In this section we are going to look at 2 types of data, in a 'use once' fashion, that of numbers and words (strings),
and then we'll look at 'keep it around and manipulate' or variables, and how we can structure that
data within the program, so it can represent tables and sources of data.

We will be using some different functions. Please ask if I don't briefly mention what they are trying
to do, but we will look a bit more in depth about them later.
